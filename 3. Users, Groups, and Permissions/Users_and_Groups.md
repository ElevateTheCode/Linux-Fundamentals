# Users and Groups

- `useradd [username]`: Creates a new user.
- `userdel [username]`: Deletes a user.
- `groupadd [groupname]`: Creates a new group.
- **`root` user**: The most powerful user. An attacker's main goal is often to gain `root` access.
- **`su`**: Switches to another user account. `su -` switches to `root`.
